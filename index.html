Teach Kids Programming

Welcome. This site is my attempt at creating a resource for teaching children (specifically my children, but maybe yours too) basic concepts of programming. This is not intended to teach them a specific programming language (though the site is using python) or syntax. Rather the focus is on the concepts. (e.g loops, maps)

Suggestions, corrections, and discussion are welcome. The source for this site, as well as a place to submit issues is available at https://github.com/skiingwiz/kids-programming-web/

Head over to the Table of Contents to see what's available or just jump straight to Lesson 1

Enjoy!
